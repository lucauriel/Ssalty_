From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia, Lauren Tsai (born February 11, 1998), is an American visual artist, model, and actress.[2] She is best known to audiences for her appearance in the Fuji TV and Netflix series Terrace House: Aloha State. She made her acting debut in the third and final season of Legion, a television series based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name.
